Elif Gül Kurt

Elif Gül Kurt

Graduated with first-class honours with BFA in Painting from Anadolu University in 2021. In 2018, She completed Erasmus education at the Accademia Albertina di Belle Arti di Torino and was able to participate in a number of national exhibitions. Then she started with MFA in Painting from Anadolu University in 2021. In her artworks, the question is body and it is norms. According to her the body is an image that meets different concepts and is constantly turned around. Instead of treating the body as a typical figurative painting, although the subject changes the human and the body are always included in the narrative.
