Baran Kurtoğlu

Baran Kurtoğlu

The artist's works, with their blurred, fragmented and layered state, are combined with an effort to make sense that seems to be insignificant in the memory and triggers some moments. It examines the state of being trivial, mundane, storyless as opposed to grand narratives. It focuses on what is remembered in the "now", away from the simplicity of the past in memory. He asks the viewer, What kind of illusion does the past find when the present is produced in it?
