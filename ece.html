Ece Büşra Eryılmaz

Ece Büşra Eryılmaz

She was born in 1994 in Izmir. She completed her undergraduate education in Dokuz Eylül University, Faculty of Fine Arts, Painting Department in 2016. In 2019, she graduated from Dokuz Eylül University Fine Arts Institute Painting Department Master's program. In the same year, she started the Proficiency in Art program at Ondokuz Mayıs University Graduate Education Institute, Art and Design Department. She also works as a research assistant at Ondokuz Mayıs University, Faculty of Fine Arts. Concentrating on the 'Entropy' series, which she made within the framework of the concepts of order and disorder, and the Legacy of Ancient Anatolian Civilization in her recent paintings, she aims to construct a narrative / mythological depth with the interaction and transfers between the works.
